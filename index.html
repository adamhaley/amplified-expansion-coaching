Meet Jena Carpenter


A modern day mystic, bridging the world of the physical and the world of the cosmic consciousness with a goal of awakening her clients & community members to a higher awareness of the possibilities & potentials that this life offers them. My goal is to guide my clients on a path of awareness, awakening, & abundance to help them understand their soul mission & move forward onto their aligned path as effectively as possible.

With over 20 years of experience as a teacher, coach, writer, & leader, as well as 20 years in the entrepreneurial world as an Aerialist, Contortionist, Yogi, and Entrepreneur, I use wide variety of tools & techniques to facilitate this process. My education includes a Bachelor of Arts from the renowned Oberlin College, and certifications in many other modalities including as a Life Mastery Consultant from the Brave Thinking Institute, and as a Yoga Teacher & Transformative Breathwork Facilitator.

I look forward to getting to know you, and supporting you in your mission to live an Empowered, Abundant, and Aligned Life. Please feel free to check out my current offerings, or book a complimentary Strategy Session to learn more about how I can support you, wherever you are on your path.
